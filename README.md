# Enzyme Metrics Pipeline: Structural & Thermodynamic Analysis of Protein Complexes

This repository contains an automated Python pipeline (`Enzyme_Metrics.ipynb`) designed to perform comprehensive structural, thermodynamic, and geometric analysis of enzyme-protein-ligand complexes. 

The workflow evaluates 3D structural models generated by **AlphaFold 3**.

## Pipeline Workflow & Metrics Calculated

The script automates the processing of input PDB structures through five core points:

1. **Structure Optimization & Cleaning (MDAnalysis)**
   Isolates the core protein-ligand system and filters out any computational or crystallographic artifacts to ensure clean and precise interface calculations.

2. **Thermodynamic & Interface Energy Analysis (PRODIGY)**
   - **Predicted Binding Affinity ($\Delta G$):** Evaluates the thermodynamic stability (in $kcal \cdot mol^{-1}$) of the CYP-POR assembly.
   - **Predicted Dissociation Constant ($K_d$):** Determines the binding strength and complex stability at equilibrium.

3. **Geometric Surface Analysis (FreeSASA)**
   - **Buried Surface Area (BSA):** Measures the solvent-accessible surface area ($\mathring{A}^2$) shielded from water upon complex formation, serving as a proxy for structural and geometric fit.

4. **Ligand Binding Evaluation (AutoDock Vina)**
   - **Docking Affinity Score:** Scores the energetic and geometric fit (in $kcal \cdot mol^{-1}$) of the ligand within the active catalytic pocket.

5. **Residue-Level Proximity Mapping (Biopython)**
   - **Contact Mapping:** Generates an explicit list of specific residue pairs actively stabilizing the protein-protein interface, providing high-resolution targets for future mutational studies.

## Purpose & Application

This pipeline acts as a high-throughput *in silico* structural screening tool. By cross-referencing AlphaFold 3 predictions with multi-tool physicochemical metrics, it enables researchers to rank different ligands based on their allosteric effects on enzyme-reductase assembly prior to wet-lab validation.
